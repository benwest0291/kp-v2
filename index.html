LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm Kelly. I'm a fully qualified and experienced beauty therapist. I gained my initial NVQ training in 2004 and have attended further continuous training, workshops and gained many qualifications ever since. I love meeting new people and delivering great treatments, I pride myself on being meticulous, professional, friendly and I really just love a cuppa tea and chat too. My beauty room is bright, airy and relaxing. I am based in Spirit Hairdressing on the gorgeous High Street of West Malling in Kent.
